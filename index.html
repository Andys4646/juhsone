Materials

Honest wood,
chosen by hand

We work mostly in two woods, with the occasional imported timber when a piece asks for it. Nothing is veneered, nothing is hidden — what you see is solid all the way through.

  • Ash

    Pale, springy and bright-grained — light to lift, strong to keep.

  • Walnut

    Deep chocolate tones with ribbons of figure that warm over time.

  • Imported timbers

    Maple, oak and rarer woods, brought in for commissions that deserve them.

The Craft

From plank to keepsake

  1. 01

    Select

    Boards are read for grain and figure. Only the calmest, cleanest cuts make it to the bench.

  2. 02

    Shape

    Hand planes, gouges and patience. The form is coaxed out slowly, checked by eye and by hand.

  3. 03

    Finish

    Sanded through the grits, then fed with natural oils and wax until the surface glows.

The Atelier

One maker,
one bench

Juhsone began with a simple urge — to make things by hand and make them beautifully. Every object is drawn, cut, shaped and finished by one person, in one small workshop. There is no production line. There is only the wood, the tools, and the time it takes to do it right.

If you would like something made for your home, I would love to hear what you have in mind.
